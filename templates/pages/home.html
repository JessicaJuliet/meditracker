About

MediTracker provides a secure, safe and convenient way to keep a log of your medical symptoms between medical appointments. Register today to create a free online account and start keeping track of your medical symptoms to share with your medical team at your next appointment.

MediTracker was founded in 2021 following the disruptions which COVID-19 brought to healthcare systems worldwide. Our Vision is to empower chronic illness patients to take control of their health and our Mission is to provide patients with a secure web application which allows them to record their medical data.
